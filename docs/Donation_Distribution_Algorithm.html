Abstract

This paper formalizes the donation distribution algorithm for an issue-based political donation platform. Donors contribute to specific issues, and funds are allocated monthly to candidates based on donor ratings. The algorithm ensures equitable distribution, compliance with campaign finance laws, and handles donation caps. 1. Introduction

The donation platform organizes issues in a tree structure, where donors fund specific (leaf) or general (root) issues. Candidates subscribe to leaf issues, and donors rate them monthly on a 1â€“10 scale. Funds are distributed proportionally based on ratings, adhering to federal and state donation caps.
